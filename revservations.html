NEED Transportation?

Beginning January 1st, 2023 we will be offering EARLY transportation for guests who wish to redeem their included admission to Sea Life Park the same day as their Lūau. To book, select one of our "EARLY Transportation" options from the booking calendar.

We offer comfortable round-trip transportation from several convenient pick-up locations in Waikiki and surrounding areas for just $25/person.

Select your transportation options during checkout. Additionally, self-parking is available at Sea Life Park for $10/car.

ADA accessible vehicles are available with 48-hour advance notice.

Please email at info@alohakailuau.com to request.

What happens if it rains?
We know many people have limited time here in Hawaii to experience a Luau. For this reason, the Luau runs in rain or shine. Our reservations department will contact you if there is a cancellation due to weather. See cancellation policy.

Can I use my admission to Sea Life Park before my luau?
You can use your included admission to Sea Life Park the same-day as your Luau, or within 5 days to 10 days depending on the package you choose, excluding the Bronze package. Your admission tickets to Sea Life Park will be included with your Luau confirmation email. Admission is non-transferrable and must match the name on your reservation.

Can I sit with someone in a separate reservation?
You can enter the last names of any parties or separate reservations that you are joining at the Luau if you would like to be seated together. If you have already made your reservation and need to add this request, please email us at info@alohakailuau.com.

Disabilities or Special Accommodations
If you require special accommodations, have a wheelchair or have any other special requests - please contact us at (866) 524-5828 at least 48 hours in advance of your Luau date. Sea Life Park and the Luau grounds are ADA accessible.

Do you offer large group discounts?
We love helping you celebrate your special occasions, birthdays or life events! If you have a large group (30 people or more), please email us at info@alohakailuau.com .

Cancellation Policy
Cancellation policy is 48 hrs. Cancellations on behalf of Aloha Kai Luau will result in full refunds.
